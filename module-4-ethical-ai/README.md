# Module 3: Ethical AI Terms and Conditions

## Learning Objectives

- Understand ethical principles in AI development
- Identify and mitigate bias in healthcare AI systems
- Learn fairness, accountability, and transparency (FAT) principles
- Develop ethical guidelines for AI deployment in healthcare

## Overview

Beyond legal compliance, AI systems in healthcare must be developed ethically. This module explores the moral and ethical considerations when building AI systems that impact human health and wellbeing.

## Topics Covered

1. **Core Ethical Principles**
   - Beneficence and non-maleficence
   - Autonomy and informed consent
   - Justice and fairness
   - Transparency and explainability

2. **Bias and Fairness**
   - Identifying bias in training data
   - Demographic representation in datasets
   - Algorithmic fairness metrics
   - Mitigating bias in model development

3. **Accountability and Transparency**
   - Model interpretability and explainability
   - Audit trails and decision logging
   - Stakeholder communication
   - Error handling and human oversight

4. **Patient Rights and AI**
   - Right to explanation
   - Right to human review
   - Data ownership and control
   - Algorithmic recourse

## Deliverables

- Ethical AI framework document
- Bias assessment checklist
- Transparency and accountability plan
- Patient rights statement for AI systems
