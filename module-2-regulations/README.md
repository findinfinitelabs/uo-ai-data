# Module 2: Regulations and Compliance

## Learning Objectives
- Understand key healthcare data regulations (HIPAA, GDPR)
- Learn data privacy principles and patient confidentiality requirements
- Identify compliance requirements for AI in healthcare
- Implement de-identification and anonymization techniques

## Overview
Healthcare data is highly sensitive and regulated. This module covers the legal and regulatory frameworks governing health and dental data, ensuring students understand their responsibilities when working with patient information.

## Topics Covered
1. **Healthcare Data Regulations**
   - HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act)
   - GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
   - HITECH Act
   - State-specific healthcare privacy laws
   
2. **Protected Health Information (PHI)**
   - Understanding what constitutes PHI
   - Safe Harbor method vs. Expert Determination
   - 18 HIPAA identifiers to remove
   
3. **Data Access and Security**
   - Access controls and authentication
   - Encryption requirements
   - Audit logging and monitoring
   
4. **Consent and Authorization**
   - Patient consent requirements
   - Research vs. clinical use distinctions
   - IRB (Institutional Review Board) considerations

## Deliverables
- Compliance checklist for healthcare AI projects
- De-identification protocol document
- Data handling policy template
- Risk assessment matrix
